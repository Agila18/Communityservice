# Government Scheme Mapping

*Vanakkam Akka* is designed explicitly not to replace existing healthcare infrastructure, but to act as a **Dynamic Bridge** structurally linking unaware rural demographics into legally mandated State and National financial incentive programs globally.

## 1. Dr. Muthulakshmi Reddy Maternity Benefit Scheme (MRMBS) 
**App Feature Integration: The 40-Week Pregnancy Tracker**
The flagship Tamil Nadu scheme providing ₹18,000 to pregnant women across 5 distinct installments globally.
* *Week 12 Trigger:* Reminds the mother to register via PICME to secure the initial ₹2000 installment.
* *Month 4 (Nutrition Kit):* Specifically alerts the mother to secure the State Government Nutrition Box natively from the PHC.

## 2. Makkalai Thedi Maruthuvam (MTM)
**App Feature Integration: The VHN Outbreak / Patient Dashboard**
Tamil Nadu’s localized door-to-door healthcare delivery pipeline. 
* *Integration:* Our `village_patient_list.dart` operates fundamentally as a digitally enhanced localized tracker for the MTM structural nurses, dynamically sorting patients by AI-Risk globally ensuring Door-To-Door priority effectively matches clinical urgency constraints perfectly.

## 3. Janani Suraksha Yojana (JSY)
**App Feature Integration: Smart Risk Alerts**
National Health Mission (NHM) strategy promoting institutional delivery structures lowering maternal mortality inherently. 
* *Integration:* During `RED` or critical `YELLOW` transitions, *Vanakkam Akka* specifically triggers actionable dictation guiding users implicitly towards institutional delivery networks tracking explicit subsidies securely.

## 4. SNEHA / ASHA Worker Pipelines
**App Feature Integration: Low-Internet Tele-Consultation**
ASHA workers face overwhelming 1000:1 localized ratios naturally causing burnout specifically. 
* *Integration:* The `consult_home.dart` messaging queues automatically isolate trivial questions generating precise asynchronous automated backgrounds allowing rural workers uniquely massive localized efficiency gains over standard synchronous calling flows heavily.
